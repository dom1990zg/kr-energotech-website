Company Overview

WHO WE ARE

K&R Energotech Experts is a specialized company in the energy sector, providing professional supervision of installation and commissioning of power and distribution transformers, site managing, and everything related to High Voltage.

With a commitment to excellence, safety, and delivery in all conditions, the company ensures reliable energy solutions that meet the highest international industry standards.

Since our first projects in 2013, we have grown into a globally recognized team trusted by leading transformer manufacturers, EPC contractors, and grid operators across Europe, the Middle East, Asia, and Africa.

What We Do

OUR SERVICES

End-to-end expertise in power transformer engineering — from initial supervision through commissioning and long-term performance evaluation.

01
🔌

Transformer Installation Supervision

Professional on-site supervision of power and distribution transformer installation, both onshore and offshore. Full compliance with manufacturer specifications and international standards. Our team manages every stage from receipt inspection to final energization.

→
02
⚙️

Commissioning Services

Complete commissioning supervision including energization procedures, pre-commissioning testing, FAT coordination, and final handover documentation for power and distribution transformers. We ensure every unit is fully operational before handover.

→
03
📋

Technical Consulting & Project Management

Comprehensive technical advisory and end-to-end project management. Coordinating between manufacturers, EPC contractors, and grid operators for seamless delivery. We bridge the gap between engineering requirements and on-site execution.

→
04
🔬

Maintenance & Diagnostic Testing

Scheduled maintenance programs, electrical diagnostic testing, oil sampling analysis, and condition assessment to maximize equipment lifespan and reliability. Proactive monitoring prevents costly failures and unplanned downtime.

→
05
🛠️

On-Site Troubleshooting

Rapid-response troubleshooting and performance evaluation for existing installations. Identifying root causes and implementing solutions to restore reliable operation. Available for emergency callouts worldwide, 24/7.

→
06
🏭

HV Equipment Installation

Installation of all HV equipment including power, distribution, industry, oil and gas. Control cabinet manufacturing, switchgear installation, and substation construction. Covering voltages from distribution level up to 400 kV transmission class.

→
